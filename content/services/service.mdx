## What is Umami?
Umami is a simple, fast, privacy-focused alternative to Google Analytics.

<Callout title="Availability">Umami is added to Coolify as a one click service on the version **v4.0.0-beta.412**</Callout>

## Notes:
1. Umami takes about 1 to 2 minutes to fully start up, so give it some time.
2. While entering your domain for Umami on Coolify make sure to add `:3000` at the end of the domain.
